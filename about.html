Project

Paper Review Tool

Built a Python-based academic paper reviewing tool during a hackathon at IIT Kharagpur. The goal was to simplify the research paper review process by offering a smarter and more structured way to analyze content. While the project is still in its early phase, it reflects my initiative to explore real-world research challenges. I collaborated with peers, brainstormed solutions, and quickly prototyped features under time constraints. The repo showcases my ability to take a vague idea and build a working prototype from scratch. It’s not perfect yet—but it’s a great start and something I’m proud to have contributed to.
