AnnotateX – Smart Reviews Made Simple

AnnotateX is an open-source web app that streamlines academic paper reviews through collaborative PDF annotation. Built with Django and React, it allows users to upload papers, highlight content, and add threaded comments for detailed feedback. The platform supports real-time collaboration, making it ideal for research groups, peer reviewers, and educators. Secure authentication ensures only authorized users can review and comment. Its clean, interactive UI enhances the reviewing experience. Whether for academic peer review or classroom analysis, AnnotateX simplifies feedback and boosts engagement.
